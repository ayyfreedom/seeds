WEB3 RESOURCES

SEEDS Entity is set to provide students across major public schools in Akwa Ibom State with basic and advanced knowledge of blockchain technolgy, and Web3.0 making it easier for them to adapt to blockchain technolgy at a very early stage...

LEARNING RESOURCES

SEEDS Entity is set to provide advanced learning infrastructure to public schools through fundings gotten from donations, e.g quality libraries,computer labs,and other cutting edge infrastructure to enable them have an edge in a rapidly changing ecosystem and also have basic knowledge of how to interact with a computer system...

COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT

SEEDS is building a movement of Web3.0 enthusiasts from public secondary schools,empowering students with blockchain knowledge to invest and give back to their communities. Through SEEDS, Web3.0 brands gain a direct channel to inspire and connect with the next generarion of tech-savvy changemakers...

MATERIAL RESOURCES

SEEDS which is the acronym for (SUPPORTING EDUCATION THROUGH EXCHANGE AND DONATION SERVICES) is an Entity set to provide material resources like Books,Shoes,Backpacks and other learning materials for students who are incapable of affording them accross public schools in Akwa Ibom State...
